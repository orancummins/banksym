Consent Journey (AIS)

1
Request Account-Access Consent
The TPP asks the bank (ASPSP) for the PSU's permission to read account data. No credentials are collected here.
2
Authorise at Your Bank (OAuth Redirect)
The TPP redirects the PSU to the bank's own login page. The customer authenticates there and approves the consent — the TPP never sees the password. The bank returns an authorization code, which the TPP exchanges for an access token.
3
Access Account Information
With a valid consent the TPP lists accounts and reads balances & transactions.

Account Information
